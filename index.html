Chenglei Si

Hi! I am an undergraduate at the University of Maryland where I am advised by Prof. Jordan Boyd-Graber. I started doing research in NLP during high school thanks to the guidance of Prof. Min-Yen Kan. I took a gap year after high school interning at iFLYTEK, hosted by Yiming Cui. Right after that, I spent my freshman year visiting THUNLP working with Prof. Zhiyuan Liu.

My research interests include: 1) Question Answering: How do we properly evaluate the performance of QA models? Can we understand on what cases our QA models would succeed or fail, and why? 2) Robustness and Generalization: How to defend against adversarial attacks? How to stress test and improve the robustness of QA models? 3) Language Representation: What is the optimal granularity for language representation? Can we develop more tailored techniques for languages beyond English that are more efficient and robust?
